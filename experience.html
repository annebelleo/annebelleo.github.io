Hugh O'Brien Youth Leadership - Ambassador

June 2016

After being nominated by a teacher, submitting an application, and going through an interview process, I was selected to represent my school at the HOBY Youth Leadership Seminar, held at Chapman University in June of 2016. Through the several presentations, group activities, and panels with guest speakers, I have learned so much about what it means to be a leader and the responsibilities that come with the position.
